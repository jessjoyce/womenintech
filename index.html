Womxn in Technology in Technology is a Slack community open to any female-identifying, genderqueer, gender fluid or non-binary persons who work in the tech sector.

We are developers, marketers, product specialists, strategists, content curators, internet builders - open to anyone working in technology or interested in working in technology.

We have our roots (and started) in Toronto, Ontario, Canada but we are also internet residents and welcome users from all over the world.
